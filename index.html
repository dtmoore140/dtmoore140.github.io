About


        Dillon Moore is a multimedia artist and developer currently living in the Orlando area. He strives to create amazing work, no matter the media it is made in. Dillon Graduated with a Bachelors of Science from Stetson University in 2015.

        A modern day "Jack of all trades" Dillon works with audio, websites, video, images, computer music, and programming to create wonderful projects.

        In his free time Dillon enjoys playing, modding, and speedrunning video games. Most recently Dillon created a Mod for The Binding of Isaac: Afterbirth+ that was released officially in the game on the PC, and will soon be added to the Nintendo Switch version of the game.
